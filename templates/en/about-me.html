Patrizio Buonaiuto

Patrizio Buonaiuto was born in Naples in 1968. In 1990/91, he graduated in Painting from the Academy of Fine Arts of Naples and since then has embarked on an intense painting career, exhibiting his works in numerous group exhibitions.

Since 1992, he began to specialize in "Author Reproduction," which is the faithful reproduction of past works, and exhibited in numerous Italian cities for leading gallery owners in the field, including "Le Musee imaginaire," "Euromedia," and "La Galleria dei Sogni." What sets him apart from most painters who engage in reproductions is his incredible versatility. His technical knowledge allows him to reproduce any painting, from classics to impressionists, to modern masterpieces, respecting the forms and methods of the original technique.

Over the years, he has created oil works and trompe l'oeil for important shops and restaurants in Naples, including: Marinella, Sartoria Sabino, Ristorante "Zi' Teresa," "La Cantinella," etc.

He has also collaborated with important galleries in London, including the "Alexander Miles Gallery," for which he has exhibited and sold several original works.
